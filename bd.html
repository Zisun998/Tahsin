A SHORT OVERVIEW OF BANGLADESH

 

Morobe Development Foundation Inc. aims to help young people reach their full potential, utilize these talents in the wider community, and become role models for other young people. During Covid-19 lockdown I was looking for an opportunity to help others and volunteer my services to the people who needs them the most. I reached out to Morobe Development Inc. who amongst others, were battling agains Covid-19 by making educational videos for the kids in lockdown. They told me to represent my own country in a short video. It was a very exiting project as I was animating what I saw all my life with my own eyes. Also this is one of the very few videos that I've animated above 3 mins long.

Client: Fiona Cruyt
Concept, Script, Illustration, Sound and Animation: Tahsin Alam
Donated To: Morobe Development Foundation
