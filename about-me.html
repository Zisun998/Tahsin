ABOUT ME

Hello I'm Tahsin. I am currently a undergraduate student studying Electrical and Electronic Engineering in one of the most prestigious university of Bangladesh, Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology. I successfully passed both my Higher Secondary Examination and Secondary School Certificate with GPA-5.00.

So, I have a background in Engineering and I love it. But I consider myself at a intermediate level when it comes to the field of motion graphics and graphics design. For the last 5 years I've always been creating something new and upgrading my skills to the point where I'm at now. I'm also an Adobe Certified Associate in After Effects and Photoshop.

I use a broad spectrum of programs including After Effects, Illustrator, Photoshop, Xd, Premiere and also Dreamweaver. I am a generalist with a wide range of skills. From designing to animating or ui/ux designing to coding, I usually illustrate and animate short humorous videos and .gifs to create tiny stories. I enjoy condensing ideas down to their simplest form and crafting them into a concentrated dose. I live to create the most organized projects files and believe in process above all else. UI/UX design and webpage design is the recent region of interest of mine at which day by day I'm getting better at.

I'm available for freelance work and would love to collaborate on any upcoming project. I worked on projects for Will trippley foundation, Morobe Development Foundation Inc., Ku-a-kanaka, EA Ecoversity etc. I worked as a motion artist for some of the brilliant companies in the motion industry, such as BiTechX Motion and MNessa Limited. Hope to meet you too in the future.
